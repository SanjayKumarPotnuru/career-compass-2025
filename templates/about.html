About Career Compass

Career Compass is an intelligent web application that helps students and professionals discover suitable career paths based on their expected salary. Using machine learning, we suggest top four job roles and provide detailed insights such as qualifications, job descriptions, future scope, and location-wise salary estimates.

Whether you're just starting your journey or looking to switch paths, Career Compass aims to simplify career planning and decision-making.
